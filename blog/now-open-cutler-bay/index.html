What Yulis Does Best

Yulis specializes in luxury protective styling for textured hair. Her work is defined by clean parting, balanced tension, and styles that look as good as they feel. Here's what she offers:

  • Knotless Braids — Lightweight, seamless braids in small, medium, and boho styles with custom parting options.
  • Box Braids — Classic protective styling with clean square parts and flexible length choices.
  • Goddess & Boho Braids — Soft curl accents, polished movement, and a vacation-ready finish.
  • Feed-In Cornrows — Straight backs, stitch braids, ponytail designs, and creative parts.
  • Twists — Senegalese, passion, and natural twist styles tailored to your hair goals.
  • Bridal & Event Styling — Custom looks for weddings, birthdays, and special occasions.

Every appointment starts with a consultation so Yulis understands your hair history, desired look, and timeline. She adjusts tension and parting for comfort, and sends every client home with aftercare guidance for scalp health and nightly protection.
