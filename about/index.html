Philosophy

Beauty should never come at the cost of comfort.

Braids by Miami is built around protective styling that looks expensive, feels comfortable, and respects the client's natural hair. Yulis focuses on clean parting, balanced tension, thoughtful color choices, and styles that suit Miami's climate and lifestyle.

The tone is elevated but personal: a calm appointment, a clear plan, and a finish that helps each client feel put together before they walk out.
